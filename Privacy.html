Privacy Policy for AUCTV App

Last updated: April 12, 2026

AUCTV App is the mobile application for AUCTV, the student-run television channel at The American University in Cairo (AUC). Operated under the Kamal Adham Center for Television and Digital Journalism, the app provides students, faculty, and viewers with access to exclusive news, programs, creative productions, featured content, and insights from AUC’s aspiring journalists and media professionals.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information through the AUCTV App.

Use of the App

The AUCTV App is designed to provide access to AUCTV content, including live and featured media. It is intended for informational and viewing purposes only.

Third-Party Links and Services

The app may include links to third-party platforms such as YouTube, Facebook, and Instagram in order to connect users to AUCTV’s official social media channels. If you choose to visit these external platforms, their own privacy policies and terms will apply.

Cookies and Tracking

The AUCTV App itself does not use cookies or tracking technologies to collect personal information.

Children’s Privacy

The AUCTV App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children or other users.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the AUCTV App, please contact AUCTV or the Kamal Adham Center for Television and Digital Journalism at The American University in Cairo.
